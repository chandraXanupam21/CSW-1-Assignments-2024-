Linear Motion Equation

S = vit + 1/2at2 : The Equation of Linear Motion

The formula S = vit + 1/2at2 describes the motion of an object under constant acceletation. Here-

  • S represents the displacement,
  • t is the time
  • vi is the initial velocity,
  • a is the acceleration, and
  • 1/2at2is the additional displacement dur to acceleration.
Note:This equation was originally written in 1905.
